Sporting Welcome to the Lions Den SCP Sporting Clube de Portugal, otherwise referred to as Sporting CP or simply Sporting, is a Portuguese sports club based in Lisbon. Having various sports departments and sporting disciplines, it is best known for its men's professional football team playing in the Primeira Liga, the top flight of Portuguese football.
